== Description ==
With <a href="http://parstools.com">Parstools</a> Social Sharing, your visitors can easily share your posts on the most popular social networks, including Facebook, Twitter, Pinterest, LinkedIn, Telegram, WhatsApp and more.You can also choose whether to display these share buttons on your homepage, category page, archive page, and other specific WordPress pages and posts.
